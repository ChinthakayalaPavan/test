Once a player deposits money and participates in a game, the entry fee is considered final. No refunds will be issued for completed or ongoing games.

3. Refund Eligibility

  • If a payment was deducted but not credited to your account.

  • If you were charged multiple times for the same transaction.

Refunds, if approved, will be credited back to the original payment method (Paytm, PhonePe, GPay, or card) within 7–10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Users can cancel participation in a game before it begins. Once the game starts, cancellation and refund requests will not be accepted.
